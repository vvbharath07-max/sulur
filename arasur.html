Comprehensive Report on Arasur, Coimbatore

Geographical Overview

Arasur is a census town located in the Sulur block of Coimbatore district in Tamil Nadu, India. The village is situated approximately 19 kilometers east of Coimbatore's district headquarters and 3 kilometers from Sulur town. Arasur sits at an elevation of 364 meters above sea level and uses the PIN code 641407.

Source: onefivenine

The area is strategically positioned along two national highways - NH381A and NH544 - providing excellent connectivity to major urban centers. The Noyyal and Bhavani rivers flow near the region, contributing to the local hydrology and supporting agricultural activities.

Source: onefivenine

Demographics and Population

According to the 2011 Census, Arasur has a population of 11,510 residents, consisting of 5,798 males and 5,712 females. The population density stands at approximately 571 persons per square kilometer when considering the total area of 20.14 square kilometers. The census town administers 3,306 houses.

Source: census2011

Key demographic indicators include:

  • Child population (0-6 years): 1,343 (11.67% of total population)
  • Sex ratio: 985 females per 1,000 males
  • Child sex ratio: 902 females per 1,000 males
  • Literacy rate: 83.38% (higher than Tamil Nadu state average of 80.09%)
  • Male literacy: 89.14%
  • Female literacy: 77.60%

Source: census2011

The 2025 estimated population is projected to be approximately 16,500.

Source: census2011

Religious and Cultural Heritage

The village's religious landscape is predominantly Hindu, with 96.29% of the population following Hinduism, while Christians constitute 2.55%, Muslims 0.24%, and other religions making up the remainder.

Source: census2011

The most significant religious site is a 200-year-old Hindu temple dedicated to Mariamman, which serves as the spiritual center for Arasur and surrounding villages. This temple hosts a major festival every five years, bringing together devotees from 12 surrounding villages for celebrations. Additionally, there is a Sri Maha Varahi temple in the area.

Source: instagram

Infrastructure and Connectivity

Transportation

Arasur benefits from excellent connectivity through multiple transportation modes:

  • Road Network: The village is connected via NH381A and NH544, with recent infrastructure improvements including four-laning projects. The Tamil Nadu Highways Department has initiated four-laning of stretches including Arasur Pirivu to Vadasittur Pirivu as part of a ₹30 crore project to decongest traffic.
  • Rail: The nearest railway station is Sulur Road, located 5.4 kilometers from Arasur.
  • Air: Coimbatore International Airport (Peelamedu) is situated 11 kilometers from the village, providing air connectivity for both passengers and cargo.

Source: newindianexpress, onefivenine

Educational Institutions

Arasur has emerged as an educational hub with several notable institutions:

KPR Institute of Engineering and Technology

Established in 2009, this autonomous engineering college is approved by AICTE and affiliated with Anna University. The institute offers:

  • UG programs: B.Tech in various specializations including Civil, Mechanical, Computer Science, Electronics, and Chemical Engineering
  • PG programs: M.Tech and MBA
  • NIRF ranking: 120 in 2024
  • Strong placement record with companies like TCS, Zoho, Tech Mahindra, and Hyundai

Source: shiksha

KPR College of Arts, Science and Research

Established in 2019, this private college is affiliated with Bharathiar University and spread across 11.44 acres. The college offers:

  • UG programs: B.Sc, B.Com, BBA with various specializations
  • PG programs: M.Com, MS Data Science
  • Placement support with companies like HCL, Virtusa, Infosys, and Wipro

Source: collegebatch, collegedunia

Climate and Weather

Arasur experiences a tropical climate with temperatures typically ranging between 24°C to 36°C. The region receives moderate rainfall, primarily during the monsoon seasons. Current weather patterns show:

  • Summer temperatures reaching up to 35°C
  • Monsoon period with scattered thunderstorms and showers
  • Average humidity levels between 50-90%
  • Moderate wind speeds averaging 15-25 km/h
