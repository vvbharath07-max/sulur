About Bogampatti

Bogampatti is a Village in Sultanpet Block in Coimbatore District of Tamil Nadu State, India. It is located 22 KM towards East from District head quarters Coimbatore. 10 KM from Sultanpet. 487 KM from State capital Chennai

Bogampatti Pin code is 641658 and postal head office is Karadibavi .

Vadavalli ( 6 KM ) , Kallapalayam ( 6 KM ) , Pappampatti ( 7 KM ) , Mettubavi ( 8 KM ) , Poorandampalayam ( 8 KM ) are the nearby Villages to Bogampatti. Bogampatti is surrounded by Kinathukadavu Block towards South , Madukkarai Block towards west , Sulur Block towards North , Palladam Block towards East .

Palladam , Coimbatore , Pollachi , Tiruppur are the near by Cities to Bogampatti.

This Place is in the border of the Coimbatore District and Tiruppur District. Tiruppur District Palladam is East towards this place .
