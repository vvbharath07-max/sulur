Kalangal

Kalangal is a village located in Sulur taluka of Coimbatore district in Tamil Nadu, India. It is situated 4km away from sub-district headquarter Sulur (tehsildar office) and 21km away from district headquarter Coimbatore. As per 2009 stats, Kalangal village is also a gram panchayat.

Kalangal has its own place in the vibrant Coimbatore region. In the following sections, you’ll find details about population, literacy, households, children, caste data, area, pincode, local governance, nearby villages, connectivity, and more.

About Kalangal

According to Census 2011, the location code or village code of Kalangal is 644379. The village spans a total geographical area of 1500.58 hectares, and the pincode of the locality is 641402. Kannam Palayam is nearest town to Kalangal village for all major economic activities.

Here's a detailed summary of the Basic Population Details of Kalangal village:

  • The total population of Kalangal village is around 5,590, including approximately 2,853 males and 2,737 females, with a sex ratio of 959 females per 1,000 males.
  • There are about 500 children aged 0–6 years in Kalangal village, reflecting the young population in the village.
  • Kalangal village has 784 people belonging to the Scheduled Castes (SC).
  • The literacy rate of Kalangal village is about 69.57%, with male literacy at 75.64% and female literacy at 63.24%.
  • There are around 1,639 households in Kalangal village.
